Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Ride It Out ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application designed to help users build tolerance for discomfort through acceptance-based techniques. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

This policy applies to all users of the Ride It Out iOS application.

2. Data We Collect

Ride It Out is designed with privacy at its core. We collect as little data as possible.

Data stored on your device only

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Session history (duration, type, completion status)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Session count for free-tier tracking

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifying information.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No location data
  • No health data
  • No contacts or photos
  • No browsing history
  • No device identifiers for tracking purposes

3. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through the Apple App Store and may receive anonymized purchase data. RevenueCat does not receive your personal information from us. Please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on their data practices.

Apple App Store

Purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. Sensitive data (such as subscription status) is stored using encrypted on-device storage. We do not operate servers that store your data.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. There is no cloud backup or account recovery â€” your data exists only on your device.

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you. As stated above, we collect minimal data, all stored locally on your device.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the App.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@rideitout.app.

6. Your Rights â€” Canadian Residents (PIPEDA)

If you are a Canadian resident, the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) provides you with rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. Since data is stored only on your device, you already have full access.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Consent: We collect and use information only with your implied consent through your use of the App. You may withdraw consent at any time by ceasing to use the App.
  • Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@rideitout.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Ride It Out is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address the situation.
