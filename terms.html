2. Description of Service

Ride It Out is a mobile application that provides tools for building tolerance to discomfort through acceptance-based techniques. The App includes:

  • Feel It: A timed session with a minimal interface designed for sitting with discomfort.
  • Breathe It: A breathing pacer with guided breathing patterns.
  • Face It: Acceptance-based messages for moments of acute distress.

The App is a self-help tool. It is not a medical device, therapy program, or clinical intervention.
